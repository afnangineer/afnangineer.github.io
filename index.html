Hello World, I'm Afnan.

The Arabic origins of my name translate to "the branching of trees". I like to think of my name as my life's metaphor. I never stop growing, I continue to add width to my trunk with new knowledge, experiences, and human interactions--to extend new branches by immersing myself in things that interest me (i.e. all things automotive, nonfiction, languages, playing piano, sustainable retail, health and longevity, Russian literature). My inquisitive nature is deeply rooted and provides the fuel for my growth as an engineer and a person.

B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Colorado at Boulder. Concentrations in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Space. Graduate coursework in Computational Fluid Dynamics, Complex Flow Physics, and Fluid Dynamics completed towards M.S. in Mechanical Engineering.
