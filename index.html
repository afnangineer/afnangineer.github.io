Hello World, I'm Afnan.

The Arabic origins of my name translate to "the branching of trees". I like to think of my name as my life's metaphor. I never stop growing, I continue to add width to my trunk with new knowledge, experiences, and human interactions--to extend new branches by immersing myself in things that interest me (i.e. all things automotive, nonfiction, languages, playing piano, sustainable retail, health and longevity, Russian literature).
